# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, constructive, technically honest, and considerate of the data
safety consequences of storage changes. Accept responsibility for mistakes and
focus review discussions on evidence and the work.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards. Until public project contact details are selected, report conduct
issues privately to a repository maintainer through the hosting platform. A
public release must replace this temporary contact route with the approved
project contact before publication.
